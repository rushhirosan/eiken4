Buñol is a small town in Spain with a long history. Only
around 10,000 people live in Buñol, and it is a peaceful town most
of the year. However, for one day of the year, the number of
people in the town increases by over 20,000. People from all over
the world go to Buñol to celebrate a tomato festival called La
Tomatina and throw tomatoes at each other.
La Tomatina began in an interesting way. In 1945, there was a
parade happening in Buñol, and musicians were performing in the
street. Some kids pushed a musician, and they stopped the parade.
The musician got angry at the kids, and then someone threw a
tomato. Many people started throwing tomatoes at each other. They
had fun and tried to throw tomatoes again the next year. However,
the government did not like this new event and stopped it for a
while.
