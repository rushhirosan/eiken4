問題1:
友人 James から次のメールをもらいました。読んで英文で返信メールを書きなさい。James の 2 つの質問（下線部）に答えること。返信の語数の目安は 15～25語。（名前は Best wishes, の後には書かない）

Hi,
Thank you for your e-mail.
Your friend tells me that you are growing potatoes and carrots in your garden at home. I have some questions for you. When did you plant the potatoes? And how many carrots are you growing?
Your friend,
James

Hi, James!
Thank you for your e-mail.

【参考解答】
I planted the potatoes this March. I am growing about twenty carrots. I like growing potatoes and carrots.
