No.1:
M: Where we're you yesterday?
W: I went to the doctor. What did you do in English class?
M: We sang some songs.
W: That sounds fun.

Question No.1: 
What did the girl do yesterday?

1. She went to the doctor.
2. She went to school.
3. She had a singing lesson.
4. She listened to the radio.

【正解1】
1. She went to the doctor.

【解説1】
放送文

W: I went to the doctor. What did you do in English class?（医者に行ったよ。英語の授業では何をしたの？）
M: We sang some songs.（歌を何曲か歌ったよ。）
W: That sounds fun.（楽しそうだね。）
Question: What did the girl do yesterday?（女の子は昨日何をしましたか？）

女性が「I went to the doctor（医者に行った）」と言っているので、1「She went to the doctor.（医者に行った。）」が正解です。
2「She went to school.（学校に行った。）」・3「She had a singing lesson.（歌のレッスンを受けた。）」・4「She listened to the radio.（ラジオを聞いた。）」は会話に出てきません。
