本文3
Fun at the Beach Jill is from a big city in Canada. 

Every summer, she visits the beach with her family. She enjoys swimming there. 

One day last summer, she also went fishing with her father. 

They got up at six in the morning and got on a boat at seven. 

They went fishing for four hours, but they didn’t catch any fish. 

Jill didn’t like fishing. The next day, Jill went for a walk by the beach at noon. 

She met a woman with long hair there. Jill asked her, “Do you like fishing?” 

The woman said, “No, but I love surfing.” Jill wanted to try surfing. The woman said, “You should take surfing lessons first.” 

Last fall, Jill went to college. At college, she started taking surfing lessons. 

Her first lesson was in the college swimming pool. Now, Jill has a lesson every week. 

This summer, she will take a trip with her friends. They will go surfing in the sea.

問題3a:
What does Jill do every summer? 

選択肢3a:
1. She goes fishing. 
2. She goes to the swimming pool.
3. She goes to the beach.
4. She goes on a boat trip. 

【正解3a】
3. She goes to the beach.

【解説3a】
習慣として「Every summer, she visits the beach with her family」とあります。釣り・ボート・大学のプールレッスンは別の時期・出来事の話です。

問題3b:
What time did Jill and her father get on a boat? 

選択肢3b:
1. At four.
2. At six.
3. At seven.
4. At noon. 

【正解3b】
3. At seven.

【解説3b】
「got up at six」「got on a boat at seven」と並べて書かれており、ボートに乗った時刻は7時です。6時は起床時刻なので混同しないようにします。

問題3c:
Who did Jill meet by the beach? 

選択肢3c:
1. A man from a big city.
2. A woman with long hair.
3. Her father’s friend.
4. Her swimming coach. 

【正解3c】
2. A woman with long hair.

【解説3c】
「She met a woman with long hair there.」と描写されています。男性や父の知人・コーチとは書かれていません。

問題3d:
What did Jill do at college? 

選択肢3d:
1. She joined a fishing club.
2. She learned to swim.
3. She started taking surfing lessons.
4. She cleaned the pool. 

【正解3d】
3. She started taking surfing lessons.

【解説3d】
大学では「started taking surfing lessons」とあり、サーフィンレッスンが本文どおりです。プール掃除や釣りクラブ・水泳習得とは記載がありません。

問題3e:
What will Jill do this summer? 

選択肢3e:
1. Buy a new boat.
2. Get a summer job. 
3. Have swimming lessons at college.
4. Take a trip with her friends.

【正解3e】
4. Take a trip with her friends.

【解説3e】
「This summer, she will take a trip with her friends. They will go surfing」と今年の夏の予定が書かれています。ボート購入やアルバイト・大学の泳ぎの授業は別の文脈です。

During their trip, they woke up at seven o'clock. Every morning, Wendy and her mother gave food to the sheep. Wendy's father cooked breakfast for everyone.

When Wendy came home, she told her grandfather about it. She said, "Helping on the farm was fun!" She showed her grandfather some photos of the farm. Her grandfather put the photos in a photo album for her. Wendy likes looking at the photos because she really enjoyed her trip to the farm.
