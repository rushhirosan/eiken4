No.1:  
M: Alice will play in a piano concert next Sunday. Alice is happy because her mother bought her a new dress to wear on stage.

Question No.1:  
What will Alice do next Sunday?

1. Buy a new dress.  
2. Play in a concert.  
3. Help her mother.  
4. Have a piano lesson.

【正解1】  
2. Play in a concert.

【解説1】  
「Alice will play in a piano concert next Sunday（アリスは来週の日曜日にピアノのコンサートで演奏します）」とあるので、正解は「Play in a concert（コンサートで演奏する）」です。新しいドレスは母が買ったものであり、ピアノのレッスンや母を手伝う話は出てきません。

No.2:  
W: Shelly had a bad headache yesterday, so she stayed at home. This morning, she felt better, so she could go to work.

Question No.2:  
Why did Shelly stay at home yesterday?

1. She had a stomachache.  
2. She had a headache.  
3. Her office was closed.  
4. The weather was bad.

【正解2】  
2. She had a headache.

【解説2】  
Shellyは「頭痛（headache）」があったため、昨日は家にいました。他の選択肢にはそのような理由はありません。

No.3:  
W: Anna likes camping. Last weekend, she went camping with some friends. She was sad because the weather was cloudy, and she couldn’t see the moon or the stars.

Question No.3:  
What did Anna do on the weekend?

1. She saw the moon.  
2. She went camping.  
3. She met a movie star.  
4. She went to her friend’s house.

【正解3】  
2. She went camping.

【解説3】  
Annaは「last weekend」に友達とキャンプに行ったと述べています。他の選択肢の内容は記述されていません。

No.4:  
M: Last summer, I went to India. I liked the food very much, and I enjoyed learning about Indian culture. Now, I want to study about India in college.

Question No.4:  
What is the man talking about?

1. His school.  
2. His favorite food.  
3. His favorite subject.  
4. His trip.

【正解4】  
4. His trip.

【解説4】  
話し手はインドへの旅行について話しています。「I went to India」と過去の旅行を語っているため、正解は「His trip」です。

No.5:  
W: I like the color red. I have many red shirts and shoes. This weekend, I’ll go shopping and get a red bag.

Question No.5:  
What is the girl talking about?

1. Her favorite color.  
2. A new shopping mall.  
3. Her new shoes.  
4. Her vacation.

【正解5】  
1. Her favorite color.

【解説5】  
女の子は「赤」が好きで、赤い服や靴を持っていて、赤いバッグを買う予定と述べています。つまり「Her favorite color」が正解です。

No.6:

How much is the ticket to Spain?

1. $1,000.  
2. $1,500.  
3. $2,000.

No.7:

Question No.7:  
Why is the boy sad?

Where does the man want to eat next Saturday?

No.9:  
W: My family likes music. I play the violin, and my brother plays the drums. Our parents both play the guitar. I also want to learn the piano.

Question No.9:  
What does the girl play?

1. The guitar.  
2. The violin.  
3. The drums.  
4. The piano.

【正解9】  
2. The violin.

【解説9】  
彼女自身が「I play the violin」と言っているので正解は「The violin」です。ピアノは「学びたい」とあるだけで、まだ弾いていません。

No.10:  
M: I missed the 9:00 a.m. train to Tokyo, so I had to take the 9:30 a.m. train. However, I wasn’t late for my soccer practice at 11:00 a.m.

Question No.10:  
What time did the man take the train?

1. At 9:00 a.m.  
2. At 9:30 a.m.  
3. At 10:30 a.m.  
4. At 11:00 a.m.

【正解10】  
2. At 9:30 a.m.

【解説10】  
「I missed the 9:00 a.m. train... I had to take the 9:30 a.m. train」と述べているので、乗ったのは9:30の電車です。
