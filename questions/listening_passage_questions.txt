No.1:  
M: Alice will play in a piano concert next Sunday. Alice is happy because her mother bought her a new dress to wear on stage.

Question No.1:  
What will Alice do next Sunday?

1. Buy a new dress.  
2. Play in a concert.  
3. Help her mother.  
4. Have a piano lesson.

【正解1】  
2. Play in a concert.

【解説1】  
「Alice will play in a piano concert next Sunday（アリスは来週の日曜日にピアノのコンサートで演奏します）」とあるので、正解は「Play in a concert（コンサートで演奏する）」です。新しいドレスは母が買ったものであり、ピアノのレッスンや母を手伝う話は出てきません。

No.2:  
W: Shelly had a bad headache yesterday, so she stayed at home. This morning, she felt better, so she could go to work.

Question No.2:  
Why did Shelly stay at home yesterday?

1. She had a stomachache.  
2. She had a headache.  
3. Her office was closed.

【正解2】  
2. She had a headache.

【解説2】  
Shellyは「頭痛（headache）」があったため、昨日は家にいました。他の選択肢にはそのような理由はありません。
