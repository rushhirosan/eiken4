No.1:
M: Where we're you yesterday?
W: I went to the doctor. What did you do in English class?
M: We sang some songs.
W: That sounds fun.

Question No.1: 
What did the girl do yesterday?

1. She went to the doctor.
2. She went to school.
3. She had a singing lesson.
4. She listened to the radio.

【正解1】
1. She went to the doctor.

【解説1】  
男性が「Where were you yesterday?（昨日どこにいたの？）」と尋ねたのに対し、女性は「I went to the doctor（医者に行った）」と答えています。
したがって、昨日彼女がしたことは「医者に行ったこと」です。他の選択肢は会話の内容と一致しません。

No.2:
W: You weren’t in class yesterday, Mark.  
M: I had a cold, Ms. Stevens.  
W: Do you feel better today?  
M: Yes, thank you.

Question No.2:  
What happened to Mark yesterday?

1. He didn’t do his homework.  
2. He lost his bag.  
3. He had a cold.  
4. He got a bad grade.

【正解2】  
3. He had a cold.

【解説2】  
Markが「I had a cold（風邪をひいていた）」と言っているため、昨日欠席していた理由は風邪です。他の選択肢は会話の内容と一致しません。

No.3:

M: No, I’ll get red ones.  
W: Is your team’s uniform red, too?  
M: No, it’s blue and gold.

Question No.3:  
What color shoes is the girl going to buy?

1. White.  
2. Red.  
3. Blue.  
4. Gold.

【正解3】  
2. Red.

【解説3】  
「I’ll get red ones（赤いのにする）」という発言から、赤いバスケットシューズを買うとわかります。

Question No.4:
Which skirt is $40?

1. The blue one.  
2. The yellow one.  
3. The green one.  
4. The pink one.

No.10:
M: Mom, let’s have curry for dinner!  
W: No. We ate that yesterday. Do you want noodles instead?  
M: I don’t really like noodles. I want pizza!  
W: OK.

Question No.10:  
What will they have for dinner?

1. Curry.  
2. Noodles.  
3. Meat.  
4. Pizza.

【正解10】  
4. Pizza.

【解説10】  
母が最後に「OK」と言っているため、息子の提案「I want pizza!」が夕食に決まったことがわかります。
