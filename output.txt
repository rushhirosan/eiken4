Grade 4 Reading Comprehension Questions

Question 28-30: Email Reading

From: Ben Taylor 
To: Graham Taylor
Date: May 5
Subject: Golf

HI Grandpa,
How are you? I watched golf on TV last week with Mom. It was very interesting. You can play golf, right? Can you teach me? I have soccer practice every Saturday and piano lessons every Sunday morning. I do my homework on Sunday evenings, but I'm always free on Sunday afternoons.
Thanks!
Ben

From: Graham Taylor
To: Ben Taylor
Date: May 6
Subject: Of course

HI Ben,
Yes, I can teach you! How about next Sunday? I'll meet you after your piano lesson. We can go to the City Golf Course. I'll bring some sandwiches and some drinks for lunch. You should bring an umbrella. You'll need it if it rains.
See you on Sunday!
Grandpa

(28) What did Ben do last week?
1. He played golf.
2. He watched golf on TV.
3. He visited his grandfather.
4. He had lunch with his mother.

(29) Every Sunday morning, Ben
1. has soccer practice.
2. has piano lessons.
3. does his homework.
4. calls his grandfather.

(30) What should Ben bring next Sunday?
1. Some sandwiches.
2. Some drinks.
3. An umbrella.
4. A raincoat.

Question 31-35: Passage Reading

A Fun Trip

Wendy is very busy. She does homework every day, and she has many hobbies. She takes piano lessons on Wednesday evenings, and she plays baseball every Saturday. During vacations, she spends time with her parents.

Last summer, Wendy and her parents stayed on a farm in the mountains for eight days. It took five hours to get there by car. There were 600 sheep on the farm. Wendy and her parents took care of the sheep with the farmer.

During their trip, they woke up at seven o'clock. Every morning, Wendy and her mother gave food to the sheep. Wendy's father cooked breakfast for everyone.

When Wendy came home, she told her grandfather about it. She said, "Helping on the farm was fun!" She showed her grandfather some photos of the farm. Her grandfather put the photos in a photo album for her. Wendy likes looking at the photos because she really enjoyed her trip to the farm.

(31) What does Wendy do every day?
1. She plays the piano.
2. She does her homework.
3. She plays baseball.
4. She watches sports with her parents.

(32) Where did Wendy and her parents go last summer?
1. To the mountains.
2. To a big city.
3. To her grandfather's house.
4. To a new restaurant.

(33) What time did Wendy and her parents wake up during their trip?
1. At five o'clock.
2. At six o'clock.
3. At seven o'clock.
4. At eight o'clock.

(34) What did Wendy and her mother do every morning during their trip?
1. They walked to a store.
2. They gave food to the sheep.
3. They cooked breakfast.
4. They talked to the farmer's children.

(35) Why does Wendy like looking at the photos?
1. They were a present.
2. Her parents are in them.
3. She really enjoyed her trip.
4. Her grandfather took them.
